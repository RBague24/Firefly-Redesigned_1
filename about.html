About Firefly

Firefly is a protocol of health and well-being coaching — the most thoroughly tested coaching protocol in the healthcare domain, resulting in positive, statistically significant outcomes across 22 peer-reviewed studies.

Our coaching is creative, science-based, and has a high impact on our clients to connect, create, grow, and thrive.

The goal is to focus on stress which impacts many areas of our lives: interpersonal relationships, nutrition, exercise, and work. We partner with you to explore the tools you already possess to achieve your goals.

About Mary Wilson

"I am a medical social worker with over 30 years of experience, having lived and worked on both the East and West Coasts, which has enriched my perspective and strengthened my skill set. I hold a Master of Education (M.Ed.) and a Bachelor of Social Work (BSW).

I have always had a passion for health and fitness. In my youth I ran track, played basketball, soccer, tennis and was an equestrian. Presently, I weight train, practice Pilates, Barre, Spin and Yoga.

After a life-changing event, I wanted to continue helping others but in a less intense environment. I decided to transition my desire to align with my lifelong passion of fitness — for me, fitness embodies the physical, mental, and spiritual.

My health and fitness coaching focuses on all aspects of a person's wellbeing, rooted in helping clients explore the stress in their lives: work, interpersonal relationships, finances, health & fitness, and self-esteem.

Working with me will be a journey in self-exploration and discovery. My coaching will help you realize that you possess the tools within to accomplish your goals and create the life that you dream."

Mary Wilson BSW, M.Ed., CHWC
